### Beispiel 4.94
$l_1 = 100db$
$l_2 = 90db$
$l = 10 * lg\frac{I}{I_0}$

$l_1 = 100 = 10*lg\frac{I_1}{I_0} \ | : 10$
$l_2 = 90 = 10*lg\frac{I_2}{I_0} \ | : 10$
$10 = lg\frac{I_1}{I_0} \ |10^x$
$9 = lg\frac{I_2}{I_0} \ | 10^x$
$\frac{I_1}{I_0} = 10^{10}$
$\frac{I_2}{I_0} = 10^{9}$
$l = 10*lg( \frac{I_1 + I_2}{I_0})$
$l = 10 * lg(10^9 + 10^10) = 100, 4db$
